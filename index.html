My Wife's First Trip To Austalia

Proposal Park | Dandenong Beach | Otway National Park | Great Ocean Road | Shrine of Remembrance

These pictures are from 2016 when my wife, Emma visited Australia for the first time.

Proposal Park

This was taken at a park near my house where my wife and I would go to walk around the lake while she was here. In 2018 when she came back for another visit I proposed to at the same spot they were was taken.

Dandenong Beach

These were taken at a place called Danedenong Beach. I have a video of a seagull trying to eat a fish the same size as it. I was trying to embed it but I couldnt get it to work properly.

Otway National Park

These taken on the way to the Great Ocean Road. It was a national park where you have to climb down a bunch of really old steps to get to the bottom of a waterfall!

This is the only landscape picture of the waterfall. I don't know why I took all my waterfall pictures vertically.

Great Ocean Road

These are of the Great Ocean Road. It's a really cool drive, it's super windy and bendy with the cliffs and ocean on one side and a beautiful forest on the other. The rocks in the pictures are called "The 12 apostles". I think there are only 9 left due to the ocean slowly destorying them. I have some videos of the drive but, once again, I couldn't get them to embed properly.

Shrine of Remembrance

Just some pictures of my Emma and I on our way to a war memorial in the heart of the city. It's really cool inside and you can get to the top. They light these pretty cool fires every night too.
